Unit 1 AP Biology Quiz

1) What type of carbohydrate is found in the cell walls of plants?

Cellulose is found in the cell wall of plants.

2) What type of bonds hold amino acids together?

Peptide bonds hold amino acids together

3) What are the two parts of a species name?

The first part of a scientific name is called the genus. The second part of a species name is the specific epithet.

4) What is “homeostasis”? Why is it important

Homeostasis refers to the ability of an organism or environment to maintain stability in spite of changes. Its important because environment can always be changing and you have to adapt.

5) What is a dehydration reaction? What else can this type of reaction be called?

A dehydration reaction is when water is lost for monomers to bond together into polymers. It can also be called a condensation reaction.
